About Me

OK, A bit about me:    I hold an Aeronautical-Astronautical Engineering degree from the University of Washington, specializing as much as was possible at the time, in spacecraft design.   I then did a stint as an officer in the USAF for 8.5 years.   During that time I did contracting for and supervised launch crews at Cape Canaveral, FL, to launch our Secret Program satellite.   Well, not so secret now.   It's been declassified and was known as the Defense Support Program.   Basically nuclear launch notification satellites.   After that I did some intel work during the original Desert Storm then got out during the big draw down after the Berlin wall fell.   At that point, I went into computer programming and did Masters of Science work at Western Washington University.   Worked in the industry for 14+ years then got out to pursue other interests and take care of my ailing mother.   Now, 10 years later, I'm trying to get back into the Tech industry.   That 10 years out of it though is a tough sell, so I'm at the end of my first quarter at Bellevue College to get a certificate in Programming for the web and mobile devices.   It should get me at least looked at and gives me modern experience in those fields.  If all else fails, I've a mobile game I'm itching to create so we will see!
