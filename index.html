Welcome to my site!   I hope you enjoy it.
The reason this site exists is because of the requirements of the Programming 109 class.   As a result, the menu items above are prescribed by the class requirements.   "About" is of course, a bit about me;  "Coursework" is about the Programming 109 course here at Bellevue College;  "Artifacts" page holds a few of the interesting pages I've created for the class;  "Module Assignments" is a listing of the Programming 109 assignments, with links to each of them;  "Contact Me" is, as you suspect, a way to contact me, well not really but it is a form that seems to look that way, with validation;  and finally, "Personal Interests" shows a few of my interests with links etc.
Even though the site is a class requirement, I have decided to give it a bit of personal flair :-)
